Privacy Policy

This app, Triviabilities Simpsons Trivia, is a free and open source app. This service is provided by Triviabilities at no cost and is intended for use as is.

The only data that the app collects directly is the user-entered names posted to the high score lists. No other data is collected by the app.
This app makes use of a third-party service called Crashlytics, which collects basic information about your device (such as the phone model and version of Android running) in the event of a crash. To see the privacy policy for Crashlytics, Click Here.
